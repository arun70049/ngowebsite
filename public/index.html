About Global Care Foundation

Global Care Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to managing wastage and donations to help those in need. Our mission is to provide essential resources such as clothing, footwear, gadgets, and food to underprivileged communities. We believe in the power of collective action to bring about positive change and improve lives.
